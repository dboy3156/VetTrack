# VetTrack — Full Feature List

> **VetTrack** is the QR-based equipment tracking system designed for veterinary hospitals and emergency clinics. Scan, assign, and report — even without internet.

---

## Core Features

### 1. One-Tap QR Scanning
- Scan any piece of equipment with a smartphone camera — no app download required
- Instantly pull up equipment details, status, and history
- Works with any standard QR code reader built into iOS and Android
- Update equipment status, check out, or report an issue in under 5 seconds

### 2. Offline-First Reliability
- Full functionality without Wi-Fi or cellular signal
- Changes queue locally on the device when offline
- Automatic sync resumes the moment connectivity is restored
- No data loss — all actions are captured and replayed in order

### 3. Severity-Graded Alerts
- CRITICAL issue alerts surface immediately to all relevant staff
- Overdue maintenance notifications with configurable thresholds
- Sterilization reminders tied to equipment usage cycles
- Inactive equipment warnings to prevent forgotten gear
- "I'm handling this" acknowledgment to prevent duplicate responses
- Alert history log for audit and accountability purposes

### 4. Equipment Checkout & Ownership
- Each technician checks equipment in and out by name
- "My Equipment" view shows every item currently held by a staff member
- Real-time visibility into who holds what — across the entire floor
- "Return All" one-tap button for fast, clean shift handoffs
- Full checkout history per equipment item and per staff member

### 5. Role-Based Access Control
- Two roles: **Admin** and **Technician**
- Technicians see equipment status and can perform daily operations
- Admins have full visibility into equipment status, history, and reports
- Admins can add, edit, and decommission equipment
- Admins can manage users and assign roles
- Role boundaries keep floor staff focused without unnecessary permissions

### 6. Monthly PDF Compliance Reports
- One-click report generation from the management dashboard
- Covers maintenance history, sterilization records, and issue logs
- Formatted for audits, veterinary board reviews, and internal reporting
- Exportable as a downloadable PDF

---

## Equipment Management

- Add equipment with name, category, serial number, and location
- Attach a unique QR code to each piece of equipment
- Print QR code labels directly from the admin panel
- Track equipment status: Available, In Use, Maintenance, Decommissioned
- Assign equipment to rooms, departments, or locations
- Full audit trail of status changes with timestamps and actor names
- Equipment notes and issue flags visible on scan

---

## Alerts & Notifications

- Real-time alert feed on the management dashboard
- Severity levels: Critical, Warning, Info
- Alert types include:
  - Equipment issue reported
  - Overdue maintenance
  - Sterilization reminder
  - Equipment inactive too long
  - Checkout overdue
- Acknowledgment system so teams know who is handling each alert

---

## Dashboard & Analytics

- Live equipment status overview across the entire hospital
- Filter by status, location, category, or assigned staff
- Activity feed showing recent scans, checkouts, and updates
- Equipment utilization summary
- Open issues and overdue maintenance count at a glance

## Shift Management

- Technician-specific "My Equipment" view for accountability
- Return All button for instant end-of-shift equipment handoff
- Shift handoff log so incoming staff know current equipment state
- No paper forms, no whiteboard confusion

## Setup & Onboarding

- Up and running in 3 steps: Print & Stick → Scan & Log → Track & Report
- No IT tickets or training sessions required
- Admin panel for adding equipment and generating QR labels
- Staff onboarding takes minutes — just scan and go
- Free to get started — no credit card or setup fee
